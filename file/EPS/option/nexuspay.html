Send Money Process

1. Open the NexusPay app on your smartphone.

2. On the app's home screen, find and tap the "Scan QR" option.

3. Point your phone's camera at the NexusPay QR code displayed.

4. Confirm the payment amount.

5. Carefully enter your NexusPay PIN to authorize the payment.

6. You'll receive an SMS and in-app notification confirming the successful payment.
